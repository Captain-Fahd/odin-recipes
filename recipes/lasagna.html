Lasagna

This is a delicious italian delicacy, consisting of a succulent bolognese sauce with multiple layers of fresh pasta topped with immaculate, fresh mozzorella

This dish is very simple to make and is sure to make your wife jealous, wink wink.

The Ingredients required:

  • Meat (minced)
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Cans of crushed tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Sugar (just a little)
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
  • Italian herbs
  • Fennel seeds
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Ricotta and Mozzorella
  • Eggs

Now lets make it:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake!
  6. Rest the Lasagna, then devour
